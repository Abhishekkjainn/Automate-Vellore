Fare Benefits and Conditions
  • The maximum occupancy limit for a single auto-rickshaw is two individuals. In the event that the number of passengers exceeds two, an additional charge of INR 50 per person shall be levied.

  • An additional fee of INR 50 will be applied to the fare for passengers who require pick-up services from the hostel gate.

  • The maximum permissible capacity for a single auto-rickshaw is limited to five individuals. Exceeding this prescribed limit is not allowed.

  • Please note that parking charges, if applicable, are not included in the fare and will be charged separately.
